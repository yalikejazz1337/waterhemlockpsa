I Will Kill You

The water hemlock is known for being deadly to humans because of the deadly toxins released by it. Eating it can be lethal, and this is a big problem because it looks similar to other edible plants like the wild carrot or Queen Anne’s lace. A thorough guide on how to identify it can be foundhere.
